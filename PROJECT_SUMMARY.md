## 🎯 Problem It Solves

**The Greenwashing Crisis:**
- 79% of green claims are false/misleading
- 71% of consumers have encountered greenwashing
- 84% want to buy sustainable, but only 27% actually do
- 63% lack information to verify claims at point of purchase

**The Innovation:**
EcoVerify reduces verification time from **10-15 minutes to 30 seconds**, enabling consumers to make confident sustainable choices.
